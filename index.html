About Me

My name is Connor Kormos. I was born and raised in California, attained a Bachelor's degree in History from the University of California, Los Angeles and am now an aspiring full stack web developer. Currently based in Reno, Nevada, I spend most of my time learning the tools required to successfully navigate this rapidly expanding industry, embracing every challenge that presents itself along the way. Next up in my professional career is the Software Engineering program offered by the Flatiron School. I am looking forward to all it has to offer and the career path I am beginning.
